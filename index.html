Who I Am

I develop web applications and small-scale Python tools — partly for fun, and partly to make people's lives a little easier. I have a particular interest in education tech; with many educators in my family, I understand the problems teachers face better than most.

My goal is to grow into a full-fledged software engineer, working in a team on applications that genuinely help people. I'm most drawn to work where I can see a direct impact on real users — especially educators trying to do more with less.

Lately I've been exploring AI-assisted development: turning simple ideas into working products quickly, then spending real time on iteration, debugging, and security review. I see AI as a tool for growth, not a shortcut — and I'm trying to stay ahead of how the industry is changing rather than fall behind it.
